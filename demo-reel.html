Snow White and the Huntsman

Groom · LookDev Supervisor

Supervised the groom and look development on the Academy Award–nominated feature. The reel highlights the creatures and environments I oversaw, along with the work I delivered on the show.

Mr. Popper's Penguins

Groom · LookDev Supervisor

Another senior LookDev artist and I were tasked with completing an entire gentoo penguin in one month, so we split the work down the middle. I handled eyes, mouth, feet, skin, and the fur on the wings and tail feathers.

The Cabin in the Woods

Lighting TD

Worked on the elevator shot for nine months, using a combination of Massive crowds and hero elevator streams filled with 2D and 3D creatures. I also lit two of the dragon-bat sequence shots.

Knight and Day

Senior Groom · LookDev Artist

Groomed all of the bulls for the show — a main hero bull plus eight variations, four of which had entirely different grooms — completing them within a two-month period.

Cirque du Freak: The Vampire's Assistant

Senior Groom · LookDev · Lighting Artist

Set up the Skinny Man, four of the tiny zombies, and several character props, starting by developing the look of the Skinny Man. For the zombies I handled look development on the skin — heads, hands, and feet (a separate artist did the robes) — and helped develop the lighting for the Skinny Man sequence.

The Incredible Hulk

Lead Groom · LookDev Artist

Handled the initial setup on Hulk, including several months of skin R&D, then groomed Hulk's hair and his torn clothing. I set up the Abomination and oversaw its development through the rest of the show. Hulk and the Abomination each had up to nine "flavors" for levels of dirtiness, each requiring different groom and texture stages for various parts of the film.
